Call for Papers

The conference aims to assemble researchers and scholars from various disciplines in order to communicate their research outputs and re-lated achievements on science, Engineering and Humanities. Regular papers, Posters, & Plenary papers will be accepted to make the confer-ence more compressive, enlightening and enjoyable event.

Researchers, Academicians, Postgraduate students, Policy makers from academia, research institutions, industries, NGOs, and other Government bodies are invited to submit Full Papers until within the sub-themes indicated below.

Sub-themes:

  • Environmental and Sustainable Energy
  • Material Sciences and Nanotechnology
  • Artificial Intelligence and Robotics
  • High Performance Computing and Big Data Analytics
  • Information and Communication Technologies
  • Construction and Transport Technologies
  • Biotechnology and Bioprocess
  • Artificial intelligence
  • Entrepreneurship and Technology-based Ventures
  • Technology for Sustainable Development
  • Mineral Exploration, Extraction and Processing
  • Social Sciences and Humanities, and
  • Any topic related to Science, Engineering and Social Sciences.
